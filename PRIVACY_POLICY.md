# Privacy Policy for SoundShade

**Last updated:** June 14, 2026

SoundShade is a lightweight macOS utility designed to manage volume attenuation for external monitors and regulate display brightness via native APIs.

### 1. Information Collection and Use
* **No Personal Data Collection:** SoundShade is a local utility. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, usage data, or telemetry.
* **No Network Communications:** SoundShade does not connect to the internet, does not use external tracking servers, and contains no analytical code or trackers.

### 2. Local Permissions & Data
SoundShade utilizes specific macOS APIs to control system configuration:
* **CoreAudio & HAL Plugins:** The optional virtual audio driver runs locally to scale audio amplitudes. No audio streams are recorded, saved, or uploaded.
* **Display Control:** DDC commands are sent directly to connected monitor hardware.
* **UserDefaults:** Your settings (such as preferences, selected devices, and fader limits) are stored locally on your device in your user directory.
